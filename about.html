Our Story

Discover the journey behind Aushadham Organics

From Tradition to Wellness

Aushadham Organics was born from a simple idea - to bring the authentic Ayurvedic remedies that our grandmothers trusted, to modern households in their purest form.

Founded in 2018 by Dr. K N Sai Shraddha, an Ayurvedic practitioner with over 5 years of experience, our company started as a small kitchen operation making Shikakai powder for friends and family. The overwhelming positive response led us to expand our product range while staying true to our core values of purity, tradition, and sustainability.

Today, we proudly serve thousands of customers across India, helping them embrace a chemical-free lifestyle through our carefully crafted products.
